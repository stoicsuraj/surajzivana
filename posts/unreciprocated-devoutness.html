Mar 28, 2026

people say let go,
as if it was that easy.
the atman wrenching conviction,
interpreted to be breezy.

but one just soberly can't,
and the lone never will.
a man in love won't budge,
he suffers there still.

he stays immobile,
doesn't dare to move.
has a heart full of ache —
relentlessly posing thyself,
what else was left to prove?

wasn't the love he flaunted adequate?
he eternally deemed it was.
he grizzles, seeking ease,
persistently staring —
her beloved flower, in his darling vase.

the floret still blossoms.
but the love doesn't arrive.
and as the seasons go by,
the man surmises —
how will he ever thrive?

prosper — destitute of her,
in his contemplation, the word plays.
he scrawls them down,
or he desolately aspires to —
for outlasting mere melancholy days.

still and all,
he exhausts his modus operandi.
he aches for her recurrence,
a wish he keeps quietly.

a bracket of artifacts —
the missus had once loved.
he attains them all,
for the sake of his belov'd.

still she won't recrudesce,
she doesn't even glance back.
still he coerced, still he lingered,
only to be put down as a shoe rack.

he dawdled in quietude,
yearning attenuating into dust.
he learned the stifled verity —
to her, he wasn't much.
